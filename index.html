About Me

I obtained my Ph.D from the Dept. of Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay, where I was jointly supervised by Profs. Subhasis Chaudhuri and Jayakrishnan Nair. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher mentored by Dr. Venkatesha Prasad. My primary research interests are networks, wireless communication, Tactile Internet, and haptic feedback. I develop communication protocols for URLLC communication to enable immersive next-generation applications such as remote surgery, telerepair, and AR/VR applications over long distances.
