About Me

I obtained my Ph.D from the Dept. of Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay, where I was jointly supervised by Profs. Subhasis Chaudhuri and Jayakrishnan Nair. Later, I worked as an Assistant Professor at the University of South Bohemia, Czech Republic. Subsequently, I joined TU Delft as a postdoctoral researcher. My primary research interests are in the area of Tactile Internet and haptics technology.
